Services

This is also a great opportunity to learn Chinese culture and travel the country. So, at A&C Global we act as a coordinator between you and the Schools in China looking for foreign Teachers. We also prepare you for what you can expect from day 1 as it pertains to culture, the work you will be doing, food, and so much more.

Who Are We Looking For?

We are always on the lookout for open minded people who are willing to learn and teach. Experience is not completely necessary, however it does help. If you are able to teach other subjects, that is always a plus....but again, not a necessity. What is most important is that you are open minded to new cultures, kind, and able to learn.

  • My mission with A&C Global LLC. is to provide opportunity to those who wish to explore living, working and learning in China. To be able to share this experience of rich culture with the world will provide the recipient a wealth of experience and knowledge. With A&C Global, we work with recruiting agencies in China that are in need of people who are willing to teach English in the school system. These schools provide the teach with generous pay, an apartment and travel cost reimbursement.
